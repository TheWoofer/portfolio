Welcome to my showoff site!

This website is just slapped together real quick as a portfolio

My name is Samuel, or the "Woofer"/"Boofer" to those that know me online. I'm a computer gamer, an artist, a game developer, a carpenter, a mechanic, a weight lifter / gym bro.. and much much more! I like to title myself a 'Jack of All Trades' due to my insatiable hunger to learn new tools and develop new skills. But to put it simply: I'm a giant nerd.
Currently, I'm working on multiple indie projects with friends of mine while I seek to start a career in the gaming design and development fields.
- 23 years old
- Current residence: North Carolina
- Open to relocation

Technical Skills

Skills that are related to computer software and hardware

- Fundamental understanding of programming
In May of '23 I began following along an online Computer Science course edX offers for free.
From this I learned the basics of C, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Since then I've experimented with GDScript and Lua while developing prototypes for games I've had in mind. By far I'm no professional in development and programming, but I'm confident enough in my abilities to further develop my skills.

- Computer assembly and maintenance
My current desktop I built from scratch in 2020 utilizing guides from online research. I do all of my own repairs and upgrades. It isn't the most up-to-date machine, but it's reliable for 3D modeling and playing modern games on reduced graphical settings!
Current Build: AMD Ryzen 5 2600 | Radeon RX 6600 | 16GB Memory

Creative Skills

My artistic capabilities

- 3D Modeling
Primary software: Blender
Within Blender I have modeled conceptual hard-surface models, such as buildings, tower defense turrets, fantasy swords, and even sculpted a few organic characters.
I've also dabbled in texture painting and rigged a few simple armatures with relatively simple IKs. Almost all of my 3d environment experience is in Blender, but I'm eager to try other software!

- Digital Art
Primary software: SAI Paint Tool 2.0 | GIMP
Truthfully, my skills as a digital artist are nothing to brag about, but I still have about 5 years of experience doodling around. I've sketched scenes from Dungeons and Dragons campaigns I've hosted, basic character references for 3D modeling, and rough concepts for weapons, characters, effects, and locations.

- Media Editing
Primary software: Davinci Resolve | Sony Pro | Audacity | GIMP
I've done basic video, audio, and image editing for personal use.
Mostly to create memes that I put in a touch too much effort.
